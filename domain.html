Business Cases


Geospatial AI for Industry Transformation – Google Cloud (2025)



Problem: Industries struggle with complex geospatial data and lack interactive analysis tools.


Solution: Google Cloud 2025 combines Vertex AI, Earth Engine, Big Query,Google Cloud and Google Maps enabling AI-driven insights through chatbot interactions.


Impact: Enhances decision-making in urban planning, disaster management, and logistics by improving spatial awareness.


Improvement Idea: Integrate ARCore to render 3D geospatial data and visualizations based on live data, along with predictive analytics for future scenarios.


Agent2Agent (A2A) Protocol – Google Cloud (2025)



Problem: Enterprises struggle to orchestrate multi-agent systems across different platforms and frameworks, leading to fragmented AI workflows and poor interoperability.


Solution: Google Cloud introduces the A2A protocol to standardize how autonomous agents communicate, regardless of their underlying model or framework.


Impact: Unifies enterprise agent ecosystems by allowing agents from different vendors or architectures to work together seamlessly, improving automation, modularity, and flexibility in AI deployments.


Improvement Idea: Introduce inter-agent knowledge sharing protocols using federated learning and reinforcement learning to enable dynamic and decentralized collaboration and adaptation.


No-Code Agent Development Kit – Google Agentspace Enterprise (2025)



Problem: Organizations face challenges automating workflows without programming expertise.


Solution: Google Agentspace enables users to create custom AI agents with natural language descriptions for automating tasks and workflows.


Impact: Enhances productivity by allowing teams to deploy agents without writing the code, automating key business processes.


Improvement Idea: Adding pre-built templates for common workflows and enabling real-time multi-user collaboration for a single agent management.


Ironwood TPU – Google Cloud (2025)


Problem: AI inference workloads demand high performance and energy efficiency, which existing hardware struggles to deliver at scale.


Solution: Google introduces Ironwood, its seventh-generation TPU designed specifically for inference, offering significant improvements in compute power and efficiency.


Impact: Enables faster and more efficient AI inference, supporting advanced applications like chatbots and generative AI models.


Improvement Idea: Integrating Ironwood TPUs into Google Cloud services.


Google Distributed Cloud (GDC) & NVIDIA Blackwell - 2025


Problem: Many industries need powerful AI but can’t always use the cloud due to security rules or lack of internet access.


Solution: Google Cloud partnered with NVIDIA (Blackwell chips) and Dell (hardware) to bring Gemini AI to Google Distributed Cloud. This lets companies run AI locally in secure or offline environments.


How It Works: GDC brings cloud infrastructure on-premises. Gemini runs on NVIDIA’s Blackwell systems. Dell helps deliver the solution end-to-end.


Improvement Idea: Allow quick syncing between disconnected sites when internet is briefly available.
